Avocado Toast with Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Mash the avocado and spread it on the toast.
  3. Cook the eggs and place them on top.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
